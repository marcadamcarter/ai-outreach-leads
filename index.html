How It Works

1

Sign Up

Register as a City Lead. We'll ship you a Tabling Kit — flyers, QR cards, stickers, and signage — ready to use at your first event.

2

Run Events

Commit to 2–3 community events over 60–90 days. Engage your neighbors in honest, accessible conversations about AI risk.

3

Report Back

Submit a short After-Action Report within 72 hours of each event. Your feedback shapes the program and amplifies our collective impact.
